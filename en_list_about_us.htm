Al kling (tianjin) environmental protection technology co., LTD. Was established in March 2014, registered capital of 2 million yuan, is a dedicated to air to purify a product research and development and manufacture of environmental protection science and technology enterprises. Company staff have undergraduate and bachelor degree or above, 1 of them have senior engineer qualification, 1 person have MBA master's degree in business administration.

Company and the first institute of China aerospace science and industry group 102 and national environmental monitoring laboratory signed long-term cooperation agreement, the product will be to provide comprehensive performance testing by the space of 102, so as to ensure the high quality of air purification products, let users enjoy the China aerospace quality products and services.

Main contents of project: high-end residential and commercial PM2.5 air purifier research and development and production.

Mainly includes:

(1) the project design background analysis; Has been completed.

(2) air purifier market analysis and product positioning; Has been completed.

(3) the design principle of air purifier; Has been completed.

(4) design of air purifier, In progress, has completed the first generation product research and development, the second generation products are being developed.

(5) test results of air purifier, In progress, 50% completed.

(6) the design of the fresh air system and air purifier market feedback tracking (in the next 12 months).

(7) physical test analysis of fresh air system.
